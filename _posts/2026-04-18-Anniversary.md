## 🎉 Techn0tz Turns 1

One year of consistent building, learning, and sharing.

What started as a simple space to document concepts has gradually evolved into something more structured — exploring Rust through real application development, along with curated learning through quizzes and book insights.

---

## 📌 The Focus So Far

- Learning Rust through real implementations  
- Building a Teacher Assistant application step by step  
- Exploring concepts like data handling, database design, and system behavior  
- Writing with clarity — not just sharing code  
- Reinforcing learning through quizzes on Rust concepts  
- Sharing insights through book reviews  

---

## 🚀 What Comes Next

The next phase focuses on deeper, real-world features and more structured learning:

- Grades and performance tracking  
- Automated workflows like mail merge  
- Improving data consistency and system design  
- Making content more beginner-friendly and structured  
- Expanding quizzes to strengthen understanding  
- Continuing curated book reviews
